About

What We Eatin’ takes the stress out of deciding where to eat with your partner.

Simply swipe left or right on nearby restaurants — when both partners swipe right, it’s a match!

Whether you’re planning date night, brunch, or a quick bite, this app helps couples agree faster and have more fun doing it.

Features:

  • Swipe-based discovery of restaurants using Google’s powerful API
  • Real-time partner matching
  • Favorites list for future outings
  • Built-in notifications when a match is made
  • Personalized preferences
  • Ad-free upgrade option

    • No more endless back-and-forth. Find something you both love in seconds.
